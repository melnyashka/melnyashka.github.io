Introduction

Posted on Fri 09 February 2018 in About me

Hi, my name is Anna. I am a first-year PhD student in University of Cergy-Pontoise (France), currently being kindly accomodated in Laboratoire Jean Kuntzmann in Grenoble.

Subject of my PhD thesis is "Statistics for Neuroscience". To be more precise, I am occupied with the questions of parametric inference and rank of noise estimation for stochastic diffusions with a degenerate diffusion coefficient (a.k.a. hypoelliptic diffusions).
