50 shades of Metal

For those, who like opera-like female vocals: Haggard, Nightwish, Therion and Tarja. Also, there is a good song "Kristalklares Wasser" from band Coronatus (German lyrics!), which has superb vocals, but unfortunately I didn't find anything of this level in the rest of band's discography. Not very metal, but Emilie Autumn also has a great voice

Power metal: Sabaton, Powerwolf, Amon Amarth --- to use with caution, good for a workout, but repetitive.

Folk metal: Corvus Corax, Eluveitie, Korpiklaani, Arkona. Separately: Good Mongolian Folk Metal is represented by Ego Fall, The HU and Tengger Cavalry.

German-language metal: Tanzwut (essentially it is the same band as Corvus Corax, but with more industrial music), Rammstein, Eisbrecher, OOMPH. I'd put a bit separately Mantus, which is a great band with German lyrics, but it is less industrial and more lyrical (with female vocals too!), and there is also Letzte Instanz with their great Rapunzel (and that's basically it).

Pop-metal (I just don't know how else to classify those guys): The 69 Eyes, Elain and Leeaves' Eyes.
